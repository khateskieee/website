From Pandemic to Empowerment

Maartie began during the pandemic, selling work-from-home gadgets that became a hit while everyone was stuck indoors. When face-to-face classes and work returned, demand shifted, and the brand had to reinvent itself. From garments and shorts came Shertified, the first name that marked the transition into fashion.

But growth needed something more—something catchy, easy to remember, and true to the spirit of the brand. That's when Maartie Clothing was born. Inspired by the word maarte, often seen negatively, we turned it into something powerful: confidence, self-care, and fashion.

Maartie is like Barbie—confident, fashionista, and proud of who she is. With affiliates growing the community and 3 years of evolution, Maartie now stands as a brand built by women, for women.

Mission

Ikalat ang self-confidence at self-care.

Fashion isn't just about clothes—it's about how you feel when you wear them. Every piece is designed to remind women that confidence is a choice, and self-care is a lifestyle.

Vision

Our vision is to become the trusted brand for affordable streetwear and elevated basics—clothing that empowers women to feel confident in every setting.

From students in coffee shops and libraries to young adults building their careers, Maartie provides style that fits their lifestyle and inspires them to own their identity.
